Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Who We Are

Clarvix ("we", "us", "our") provides digital presence audit services, B2B lead generation, and AI-powered advertising technology solutions. Our website is clarvix.net and you can reach us at contact@clarvix.net.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, and message content when you reach out to us.
  • Audit orders: Business name, website URL, city, and preferred language.
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics 4 (page views, referral sources, device type). No personal data is collected through analytics.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies for measuring performance. No advertising cookies are used.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the services you ordered (audits, leads, AdTech systems).
  • To respond to your contact form messages.
  • To improve our website and services based on anonymous usage patterns.
  • To send order confirmations and delivery notifications.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics 4: Website traffic analysis (anonymized).
  • Google Tag Manager: Tag management (no personal data stored).
  • Formspree: Contact form processing.
  • Tawk.to: Live chat support.
  • Fiverr: Marketplace transactions (governed by Fiverr's own privacy policy).
  • Payoneer: International payment processing.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information and order data for the duration of our business relationship plus 12 months. Analytics data is retained for 14 months. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@clarvix.net.

7. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption across all pages. Client audit data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only. We do not store payment information — all payments are processed through Fiverr or Payoneer's secure systems.

8. International Transfers

Clarvix serves clients worldwide. Data may be processed in Israel (where Clarvix is based) and in countries where our third-party services operate. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
