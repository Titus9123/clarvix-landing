FAQ

Common Questions

Do you need access to my website backend?
No. The standard audit is 100% non-intrusive — we only need your website URL. Optional GA4/GSC read-only access makes the audit deeper but is not required.
How long does the audit take?
Basic plan: 48 hours. Standard: 5 days. Premium: 3 days (priority). The clock starts when you provide your business info, not when you pay.
What markets and languages do you serve?
We serve businesses in English (US, UK, AU), Spanish (LATAM, Spain), Hebrew (Israel), and Arabic (Israel, MENA). Reports are delivered in your preferred language.
Can you help implement the audit recommendations?
Yes. We offer implementation add-ons: Tracking Fix Pack ($79–$149), GBP Quick Fix ($59–$99), and Speed Quick Wins ($79–$149). Ask us when you order.
What makes Clarvix different from a regular agency?
We don't do opinions. Every finding has verifiable evidence, a business impact in numbers, and a concrete fix. The Lead Conversion Score is consistent and comparable. No fluff.
Do you work outside Fiverr?
Yes. You can order directly from this page — especially for Lead Generation and AdTech AI projects. We accept international payments via Payoneer (from any country), bank transfer, and Fiverr. Contact us and we'll send you a payment request directly.
האם זה שווה את ההשקעה? מה ה-ROI?

בעיה קריטית אחת שלא זוהתה יכולה לעלות לך אלפי דולרים כל חודש. הלקוחות שלנו מחזירים את ההשקעה תוך 2-3 שבועות.
