FAQ

Common Questions

Do you need access to my website backend?
No. The standard audit is 100% non-intrusive — we only need your website URL. Optional GA4/GSC read-only access makes the audit deeper but is not required.
How long does the audit take?
Basic plan: 48 hours. Standard: 5 days. Premium: 3 days (priority). The clock starts when you provide your business info, not when you pay.
What markets and languages do you serve?
We serve businesses in English (US, UK, AU), Spanish (LATAM, Spain), Hebrew (Israel), and Arabic (Israel, MENA). Reports are delivered in your preferred language.
Can you help implement the audit recommendations?
Yes. We offer implementation add-ons: Tracking Fix Pack ($80–$150), GBP Quick Fix ($60–$100), and Speed Quick Wins ($80–$150). Ask us when you order.
What makes Clarvix different from a regular agency?
We don't do opinions. Every finding has verifiable evidence, a business impact in numbers, and a concrete fix. The Lead Conversion Score is consistent and comparable. No fluff.
Is it worth the investment? What's the ROI?
Most businesses lose $500–$5,000/month in potential revenue from fixable digital issues — broken tracking, slow pages, missing CTAs, poor local presence. Our audit costs a fraction of that and gives you the exact roadmap to stop the bleeding. One fixed issue often pays for the entire audit in the first week.
I already have a marketing agency. Why do I need this?
Your agency executes. We diagnose. Think of it like getting a second medical opinion — we give you an independent, data-backed assessment of what's actually working and what isn't. Many clients use our audit to hold their current agency accountable with real numbers.
What if my score is already good?
Great — then you'll know exactly where you stand vs. your competitors and get specific optimizations to go from good to dominant. Even high-scoring sites have 3–5 quick wins worth thousands in annual revenue.
Do you work outside Fiverr?
Yes. You can order directly from this page — for AdTech AI projects and audit orders. For quick audit orders, find us on Fiverr at fiverr.com/albertneuman402. For direct payments, we handle invoicing via Payoneer.
